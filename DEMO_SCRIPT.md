# DEMO VIDEO SCRIPT — StreamWage (~3:30, max 4:00)

Format: screen recording + voiceover. Shoot the live app on Celo Sepolia; have the employer dashboard on desktop and MiniPay on a phone (or phone mirror). Keep cuts tight. Numbers in cUSD.

---

### 0:00–0:25 · The problem (hook)

**ON SCREEN:** Title card "StreamWage — get paid by the second, in stablecoins, on your phone." Cut to a stat overlay: "1.4B people are paid late, in cash, across borders."
**VOICEOVER:** "Most of the world's workers — gig, remote, cross-border — get paid late, in cash, or lose a cut to remittance fees. They wait two weeks for money they've already earned. StreamWage fixes that: salary that streams to your phone in stablecoins, claimable the second you earn it."

### 0:25–0:45 · The solution (one line)

**ON SCREEN:** Simple diagram — Employer → cUSD vault → live stream → Worker's MiniPay.
**VOICEOVER:** "An employer funds a payroll vault in cUSD on Celo. Each employee gets a stream that accrues every second. Workers claim straight into MiniPay — no bank, no payday, no borders."

### 0:45–1:25 · Live demo, part 1 — Employer funds + onboards

**ON SCREEN:** Employer dashboard. Connect wallet. Enter cUSD amount → Approve → Fund; vault balance updates. Then "Add employee" → paste worker address → "Verify with Self" → QR → phone scans with Self app → ✅ verified.
**VOICEOVER:** "Here's the employer. I fund the vault with cUSD. Now I add an employee — but first they verify their identity with Self Protocol, so payroll only goes to a real, verified person. One scan, privacy-preserving, done."

### 1:25–2:05 · Live demo, part 2 — Create stream + watch it accrue

**ON SCREEN:** Create stream (worker + rate). The "accrued" counter starts ticking up live, second by second. Briefly hit Pause (counter freezes) then Resume (counter continues).
**VOICEOVER:** "I create a stream at a per-second rate. Watch — their pay is accruing in real time. I can pause and resume any stream. The employee can see exactly what they've earned, live, on-chain."

### 2:05–2:55 · Live demo, part 3 — Worker claims in MiniPay (the money shot)

**ON SCREEN:** Switch to phone. Open MiniPay → built-in browser → StreamWage. App auto-connects (call this out). Worker sees their accruing balance → taps **Claim** → approves → MiniPay balance increases. Show the on-chain tx on Blockscout briefly.
**VOICEOVER:** "Now the worker, on their phone, inside MiniPay. Notice it connects automatically — that's the MiniPay integration. They see what they've earned and tap claim. The cUSD lands in their wallet instantly, and here's the transaction on Celo. Money earned this minute, in their hands this minute."

### 2:55–3:20 · Why Celo

**ON SCREEN:** Three icons — Mento cUSD · MiniPay · Self. Verified contract link on Blockscout.
**VOICEOVER:** "StreamWage is built for Celo: stable value with Mento cUSD, mobile reach with MiniPay, and trust with Self Protocol identity. Contracts are deployed and verified on Celo. This is exactly the rails Celo was built for."

### 3:20–3:30 · Close

**ON SCREEN:** Logo + URL + "Streaming payroll for the next billion workers."
**VOICEOVER:** "StreamWage. Streaming payroll, on Celo, for the next billion workers."
