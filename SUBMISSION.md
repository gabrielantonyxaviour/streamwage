## Description (paste into project profile)

> StreamWage is real-time salary streaming for the next billion workers. An employer funds a payroll vault with Mento cUSD on Celo and opens a per-employee stream that accrues every second. Workers claim their earned pay anytime — straight into MiniPay on their phone, no bank, no payday, no remittance fees. Employee onboarding is gated by Self Protocol identity verification, so payroll only flows to verified people. Built mobile-first for gig, remote, and cross-border workers in the markets Celo serves.
