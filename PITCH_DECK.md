# PITCH DECK — StreamWage (10 slides)

Build as a ≤10-slide deck (Google Slides / Pitch / Tome). Each slide below = title + content + visual note. Keep one idea per slide.

---

### Slide 1 — Title
**StreamWage** · "Streaming payroll, on Celo, for the next billion workers."
*Visual:* logo, the phrase, a phone showing a balance ticking up. Celo + MiniPay marks.

### Slide 2 — Problem
Workers earn daily but get paid late.
- 1.4B+ gig / remote / cross-border workers paid late, in cash, or via costly remittance.
- They wait weeks for money already earned; fees eat 5–10%.
*Visual:* a calendar with payday circled far away; a remittance-fee bar.

### Slide 3 — Solution
Salary that streams by the second, in stablecoins, to your phone.
- Employer funds a cUSD vault → per-employee streams accrue every second → workers claim into MiniPay anytime.
*Visual:* Employer → cUSD vault → live stream → MiniPay wallet.

### Slide 4 — How it works
1. Fund payroll in cUSD. 2. Verify employee with Self. 3. Stream at a per-second rate. 4. Worker claims in MiniPay.
*Visual:* 4-step horizontal flow with the StreamWageVault contract in the middle.

### Slide 5 — Live demo
Screens: employer funds + creates a stream (accrual ticking); worker claims in MiniPay; on-chain tx on Blockscout.
*Visual:* 3 real screenshots from the app. (Or embed the demo video.)

### Slide 6 — Built for Celo
- **Mento cUSD** — stable value, no volatility for wages.
- **MiniPay** — 1-tap claim, mobile-first, reaches the actual users.
- **Self Protocol** — verified identity so payroll goes to real people.
- Contracts deployed + **verified** on Celo.
*Visual:* three logos + a Blockscout "verified" badge.

### Slide 7 — Why now / why Celo wins this
Celo is mobile, stablecoin-native, and built for real-world payments in emerging markets — StreamWage is payroll for exactly that audience.
*Visual:* map highlight (Africa/LatAm/India) + "stablecoin payroll" tag.

### Slide 8 — Traction plan
- Pilot with 1–2 small remote teams / DAOs paying contributors in cUSD.
- Onchain metric goal: real streams created + real claims (live txns), not demos.
*Visual:* simple funnel: pilots → streams → weekly claims.

### Slide 9 — Roadmap
- Now: streams + MiniPay claim + Self gate (live on Celo).
- Next: recurring payroll templates, batch funding, employer analytics, fiat off-ramp via MiniPay partners, mainnet.
*Visual:* 3-step timeline.
